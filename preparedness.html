Preparedness & Response

Household Preparedness

  • Assemble an emergency kit with water, food, medicines.
  • Create a family communication plan and a meeting point.
  • Know local evacuation routes and shelters.

Community Preparedness

  • Early warning systems and public education campaigns.
  • Volunteer training and regular drills.

Response & Recovery

  • Coordinate medical and search-and-rescue efforts.
  • Plan for long-term recovery: housing, livelihoods, mental health.

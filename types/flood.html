Floods

Overview

Floods result from excessive rainfall, river overflow, dam failure, or coastal storm surge. They pose significant risks to life, property, agriculture, and infrastructure.

Causes

  • Heavy precipitation, poor drainage, river overflow, storm surge.

Impacts

  • Displacement, waterborne disease, infrastructure damage.

Preparedness

  • Community early warning, elevate critical systems, evacuation plans.

Example

2010 Pakistan Floods: Widespread flooding affecting millions and highlighting the need for floodplain management.

Cyclones

Overview

Cyclones (also known as hurricanes or typhoons) are intense tropical storms characterized by strong winds, heavy rainfall, and storm surge.

Impacts

  • Wind damage, coastal flooding, and extended power outages.

Preparedness

  • Secure structures, heed evacuation orders, and maintain emergency supplies.

Example

2019 Cyclone Fani (India): Significant impacts prompting large-scale evacuations and response.

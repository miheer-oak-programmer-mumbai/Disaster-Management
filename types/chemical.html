Chemical Disasters

Overview

Chemical incidents can release toxic substances, posing immediate and long-term health and environmental risks.

Preparedness

  • Hazard mapping, emergency response training, and clear evacuation and sheltering guidance.

Example

Large-scale chemical releases require hazmat response units and coordinated health surveillance.

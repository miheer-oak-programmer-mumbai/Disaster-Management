Drought

Overview

Droughts are prolonged periods of below-average precipitation, causing water shortages, crop failure, and long-term socioeconomic impacts.

Impacts

  • Agricultural losses, food insecurity, and migration.

Preparedness

  • Water conservation, drought-resistant crops, contingency planning.

Example

2011 East Africa Drought: Severe food insecurity and humanitarian response needs.

Fires (Wildfires & Industrial)

Overview

Wildfires and industrial fires can spread rapidly, endangering communities and ecosystems. Smoke and air quality are major secondary concerns.

Preparedness

  • Create defensible space, maintain alarms, and have clear evacuation plans.

Example

2019–2020 Australian Bushfires: Extensive ecological and community impacts.
